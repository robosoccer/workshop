About Us

At RoboZomes, we believe in igniting curiosity and creativity in young minds through hands-on robotics workshops. Our team, made up of passionate IITians with extensive experience in robotics, is dedicated to sharing our knowledge and inspiring the next generation of innovators.


We provide children with the unique opportunity to design and build their own robots, fostering both creativity and practical skills. Our workshops are designed to be interactive and fun, ensuring that every participant, from ages 7 to 15, gains confidence in building something from scratch.


In our previous workshop, 18 enthusiastic young participants built their own robots and had a blast doing so. For our upcoming session, we are thrilled to welcome another group of 10 young minds ready to explore the world of robotics!


Join us on this exciting journey of learning, building, and innovating!
